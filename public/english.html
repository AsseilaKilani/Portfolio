ABOUT US

PRESENTATION

AMATEUR LEAGUE is a company based in organizing football events. We organize tournaments across Europe that involve both young people and adults. We are mainly located in France, which is where we are based.

OUR HISTORY

In 2023, the idea of organizing an Amateur European Cup was born. The idea came from three people: Kilani A, Beni T, and Gibril Z. Being football players and spectators for a long time, we wanted to establish a major competition for people who do not have the chance to play at a high level, giving them a glimpse of a large-scale competition and allowing them to showcase their talents. We started the initial work last year. We are based in Lens, where the project began. We found a name that immediately made sense, Amateur League. We are trying to take steps with brands and personalities to start our operations.

AREA OF ACTION

Our scope of action is wide. Our tournaments range from Paris to Lisbon, Madrid, Rome, Berlin, passing through London, Brussels, and ending in Copenhagen.

OBJECTIVE

By organizing these tournaments, we allow thousands of people to have fun, compete, test themselves, travel, and also achieve their dreams, such as aiming for a higher level. Participants must be amateurs, which makes the stakes even higher. In a world as massive and scrutinized as football, places are limited. Our goal is to develop amateur football.
